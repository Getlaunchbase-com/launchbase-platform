## 1. What LaunchBase Is

> LaunchBase compiles business intent into a credible digital artifact.  
> It is not a website builder, not a design tool, and not a branding service.

---

## 2. What "Good Design" Means

Design quality is measured by:

- **Speed to approval**
- **Reduction in edits**
- **Reduction in questions**
- **Reduction in human intervention**

**A site is "better" if it reduces doubt faster.**

---

## 3. What Design Is NOT

LaunchBase does not:

- Offer aesthetic choice
- Ask for style preferences
- Promise originality
- Allow visual revisions
- Optimize for "wow"

**If a feature increases taste, choice, or subjectivity â†’ it is rejected.**

## 5. Sections Are Compiler Contracts

- Has a **fixed purpose**
- Has **hard constraints**
- Can **fail and be omitted**
- Cannot be "styled" freely

**Violations auto-fallback to Tier 0 defaults.**

1. Appears in Tier 1
