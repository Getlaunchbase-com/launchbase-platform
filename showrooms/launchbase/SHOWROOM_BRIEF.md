# LaunchBase Showroom Brief

## Purpose

Marketing website for LaunchBase — an operating system for small businesses that handles ongoing website responsibility so founders can focus on their business.

## Audience

- **Primary:** Small business owners (1-10 employees)
- **Secondary:** Solopreneurs, freelancers, early-stage startups
- **Pain points:** Website maintenance burden, technical complexity, "carrying the system in their head"

## Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Confident, direct, empowering
- **Style:** Clear, jargon-free, action-oriented
- **Personality:** Reliable partner, not flashy vendor

## Key Messages

1. "Stop carrying the system in your head" — LaunchBase takes ongoing responsibility
2. "Your website exists. Your tools work. But no one owns the system."
3. "See your real site before you pay. Always see what we're doing."
4. Built in 48 hours, maintained forever

## Visual Identity

- **Primary color:** Orange (#FF6B35)
- **Accent:** Dark background with high contrast
- **Typography:** Bold headlines, clear body text
- **Imagery:** Browser mockups, system diagrams (not stock photos)

## Constraints

### Do Not Change
- Core value proposition: "ongoing responsibility"
- 48-hour build promise
- "See before you pay" transparency guarantee
- Orange brand color (#FF6B35)

### Must Maintain
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast load times (<2s)
- Clear CTA hierarchy
- Accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)

## Content Sections

1. **Hero:** Stop carrying the system in your head
2. **Problem:** Your website exists, but no one owns the system
3. **Solution:** LaunchBase takes ongoing responsibility
4. **How It Works:** 3-step process
5. **Pricing:** Transparent tiers
6. **Social Proof:** (placeholder for testimonials)
7. **CTA:** Hand It Off button

## Success Metrics

- Conversion rate: CTA clicks / visits
- Time on page: >30s average
- Mobile usability: 100% responsive
- Accessibility score: AA or higher
- Load time: <2s
